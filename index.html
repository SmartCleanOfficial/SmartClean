SmartClean

Official homepage of SmartClean Google Sheets add-on.

SmartClean is a Google Sheets add-on that cleans and formats spreadsheet data in one click.

It is designed for students, office workers, data encoders, virtual assistants, and analysts who regularly work with spreadsheet data.

It automatically trims extra spaces, removes multiple spaces, fixes text capitalization (capitalizing the first letter), and standardizes email formats. It works on data that is imported, pasted, or manually typed to keep spreadsheets clean and consistent.

Purpose

The purpose of SmartClean is to help users clean and organize spreadsheet data quickly and efficiently.
